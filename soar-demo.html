SOAR

Surveillance, Observation, and Aerial Reconnaissance

Project Overview

Autonomous aerial system concept for public safety

SOAR is an innovative drone technology that enhances the protection and efficiency of on-duty police officers by offering them security, observation, and aerial reconnaissance. The system utilizes AI technology to autonomously survey scenes before officers step out of their vehicles, with implementation into police car roofs for enhanced safety for both citizens and officers.
